By Logan Wright

This is a model of the Saturn V rocket built by NASA which was used in the 1960's and 70's as it was designed specifically for moon travel and lunar exploration. This model was designed and 3D printed utilizing a thrust vectoring system in an attempt to provide augmented stabililty to the vehicle via changing its horizonal and vertical accelerations by moving the motor. Tis specific rocket was designed to run on the Estes F-15 rocket motors. The flight softwae was written in C++ and was running on an Arduino Uno. CAD files are available in the projects section.

  • Launch Site: Plaster City, CA
  • Liftoff Mass: 0.920 kg
  • Software Version: 1.2
